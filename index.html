NEWNHAM COLLEGE
Gender neutral toilets: All toilets
Dress codes: Gender neutral
Training in LGBT+ issues: All staff to be trained in upcoming vacation
Bullying/harrassment policies: All government protected characteristics are covered
Name change policy: Case by case, but has been done previously
JCR LGBT+ Officer budget: £150
CHURCHILL COLLEGE
Gender neutral toilets: All except on main concourse
Dress codes: Not gendered
Training in LGBT+ issues: College nurse and counsellors are, tutors are not
Bullying/harrassment policies: Not explicit but mentions homophobic and heterosexist language
Name change policy: College well equipped to help and have done it before
JCR LGBT+ Officer budget: £800 between 4 welfare officers
CLARE COLLEGE
Gender neutral toilets: GN public toilets in Old Court, Memorial Court and the Colony
Dress codes: Not gendered
Training in LGBT+ issues: Yes, especially college nurse
Bullying/harrassment policies: No complaints
Name change policy: Senior tutor is understanding but no obvious policy
JCR LGBT+ Officer budget: £200

CORPUS CHRISTI COLLEGE
Gender neutral toilets: All undergraduate toilets
Dress codes: Not gendered, a little binary
Training in LGBT+ issues: Counsellor and harrassment officers trained, no college nurse
Bullying/harrassment policies: Mentions specifically sex, gender, and sexuality based discrimination
Name change policy: No official policy, but believes staff would be good
JCR LGBT+ Officer budget: £380 (request dependent)
DOWNING COLLEGE
Gender neutral toilets: No
Dress codes: Not gendered
Training in LGBT+ issues: No
Bullying/harrassment policies: Sexuality and gender identity explicitly mentioned
Name change policy: A formal change of name is possible and should be carried out via the Tutorial and Admissions Office
JCR LGBT+ Officer budget: Comes out of welfare budget
EMMANUEL COLLEGE
Gender neutral toilets: Student accomodation is GN, most public toilets are not
Dress codes: Gendered for graduation, neutral otherwise
Training in LGBT+ issues: No
Bullying/harrassment policies: Explicitly references both sexuality and gender
Name change policy: Difficult
JCR LGBT+ Officer budget: Reasonable expenses, otherwise approved through JCR
